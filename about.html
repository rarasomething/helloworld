Mission

Mission

Printing “Hello, world!” in a console is the first thing that people do when they learn a new programming language. Just like the meaning behind the phrase, we want to provide support for students -- no matter their proficiency in technological concepts -- to learn to stand their ground in the vast field of tech.

We aim to foster an environment where individuals passionate about technology can help improve digital literacy and accessibility. We focus on preparing students for the oncoming era and providing opportunities to utilize their talents.

Divisions

1. Communications ;

The Communications division manages our Instagram. The division members promote our club through various social media outlets.

2. VS Exploratory ;

The VS Exploratory division attends the exploratory sessions on Mondays 2:45~3:35 and helps teach the VS students block coding through Scratch. They also plan events such as VS Scratch Coding competitions.

3. Website Management ;

The Website Management division documents initiatives on our website and updates information as needed.

4. Service ;

The Service division organizes and executes various community events, collaborations, and volunteer sessions.
